Who am I?

"Great design is a merger of form and function."" This is the heart of my design philosophy. Where's my manners, how rude of me, not to introduce myself.

Hi! I'm Bertus Coetzer, but you can call me Bert. I'm a designer/front-end developer, photographer and teacher. Oh, yeah!, and a full time dad. Born, raised and corn fed in South Africa and currently residing in Thailand. My dream is to dominate the design industry and take over the world with my trusted sidekick, Pinky.

UI / UX DESIGN

UI/UX involves planning and iterating a site's structure and layout. Once the proper information architecture is in place, I design the visual layer to create a beautiful user experience.

FRONT END

Front End Development is building out the visual components of a website. Using HTML, CSS , and Javascript, I build fast, interactive websites.

BRAND DESIGN

While most of my attention is on interactive projects, I do branding and collateral from time to time. This includes logo design, business cards, and other printed materials.
